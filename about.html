About Us


In the summer of 2019 we participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. Most of us came into this program not knowing a thing about coding or computer science. We started with the basics of scratch, and slowly advanced to Python, JavaScript, CSS, and HTML. Everyday was a new experience, we had guest speakers, a field trip every friday, and multiple workshops with inspiring emerging tech leaders. These people taught us how to be brave and reach for the stars despite the obstacles in today’s patriarchal society. Through the last seven weeks, we have developed our coding skills to one final endeavor - our final project.

Our Project

We were asked to create a website, game, or use robotics to create a project of our choice. Finding the right college can be difficult and stressful. Our goal was to help students interested in attending a college in Georgia by matching them to a school through our quiz. Your match is determined by a variety of different aspects, ranging from your GPA to your ideal surrounding area.
